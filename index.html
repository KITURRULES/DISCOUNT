Frequently Asked Questions

Here’s everything you need to know about getting listed, ranked, and reviewed on DISCOUNT.

How can my company get listed on DISCOUNT?

Simply choose a partnership plan in the pricing section and sign up. Once approved, your business will appear in the DISCOUNT app where customers can discover your offers and leave reviews.

Do customers really review businesses on DISCOUNT?

Yes! Every registered user can leave a verified review after redeeming a discount or purchasing through the platform. Reviews help your business gain trust and visibility in the rankings.

What determines my ranking in the DISCOUNT app?

Rankings are based on a mix of customer reviews, engagement with your discounts, and your partnership tier. Premium Partners enjoy top visibility, but all businesses have the chance to rise through positive feedback.

Can I promote special offers or seasonal campaigns?

Absolutely! Featured and Premium Partners can run monthly or unlimited promotional campaigns. These are highlighted in the app to attract more customers during peak shopping times.

How much does it cost to join DISCOUNT?

We offer flexible plans starting from KES 0 for basic listing, KES 3,500/month for Featured Partners, and KES 7,000/month for Premium Partners. You can scale your plan anytime as your business grows.
